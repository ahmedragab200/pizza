about us

Most of our customers come in for our savoury pizza, but pizzeria italia restaurant also offers succulent home-made pasta dishes like manicotti, panzerotti, lasagne & cannelloni. With our generous portions, you’ll definitely leave satisfied! Whatever pasta you order, you’ll love our mouth-watering bread that comes with it. Family-owned & -operated since 1966, pizzeria italia restaurant is where barrie locals go for mouth-watering, home-made italian food. Whether you order our special thin-crust pizza or a meatball sandwich, you’re sure to leave satisfied.
